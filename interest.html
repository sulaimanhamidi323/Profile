My Interests

Badminton

Badminton is more than just a game for me — it keeps me active and constantly challenges me to improve. I love the energy of fast-paced rallies and the thrill of friendly competition, whether I’m smashing it in singles or teaming up in doubles. It’s not just about the sport, it’s also one of the best ways for me to unwind, connect, and laugh with friends.

Hiking

Hiking is my escape into nature. I enjoy the peaceful moments away from screens, breathing in fresh air, and discovering new trails with stunning views. It helps me clear my mind, stay active, and reconnect with myself. Whether I go solo or with friends, every hike feels like a fresh reset.
